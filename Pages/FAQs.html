Frequently Asked Questions

If you have a question that is not answered below, please feel free to Contact Us!

What are the hourly rates for the Grinds?

The hourly rate for Grinds is €30 Online and €35 for In Person.

How do the In Person Grinds work?

Upon starting grinds with a Tutor, you will be given either the house address of the Tutor, or the Tutor can travel to your house or meet in a public place, yet this will result in additional travel fees.

How much are the extra Travel Fees for the Tutor to come to my house?

Depending on the distance, Fees vary from €15-€25.
