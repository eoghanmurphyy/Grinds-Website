Job Title

Tutor

Job Description

Giving Secondary School Grinds to Students on topics such as Maths, Biology, Chemistry or Physics.

Applying

Apply by sending an email to eoghanmurphhy@gmail.com including a CV and what subject you would be willing to Tutor.

How much will I get paid and How much hours will I have to work?

The hourly rate for this role is €25 per Grind. The successful applicant will only have to work the hours of the Grind Session (Usually 1 hour per Grind). Supplementary Notes + Questions + Extra homework for each Student will be given to the tutor for each session, so no extra planning or work for any of the grinds is necessary for the Tutor other than the Grind itself.
